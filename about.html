About Us

The Inspect Research Group at the University of Insubria is a leading research team specializing in spectrometry. Our mission is to advance scientific knowledge in the field of high-resolution spectrometry through cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and innovative applications.

We work on a variety of projects that focus on atomic and molecular analysis, optical spectroscopy, and the development of new technologies for material characterization. Our research contributes to advancements in fields such as environmental science, biomedical imaging, and astrophysics.

Our Mission

Our goal is to push the boundaries of spectrometric analysis by developing new methodologies and improving existing techniques. We collaborate with international institutions, industry partners, and academic researchers to bring real-world impact to our discoveries.

By leveraging state-of-the-art instrumentation and theoretical modeling, we aim to enhance the precision and applicability of spectrometry in various scientific and industrial domains.

Collaborations & Impact

Our research extends beyond the lab through partnerships with universities, research centers, and industry leaders. We actively participate in national and international projects, contributing to global scientific advancements.

The Inspect Research Group also engages in educational outreach, training the next generation of scientists and fostering a dynamic research environment for students and young researchers.
