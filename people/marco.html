Marco Lamperti

Senior Researcher

Marco Lamperti is a senior researcher at the Inspect Research Group at the University of Insubria. He specializes in advanced spectrometric techniques and their applications in condensed matter physics.

With years of experience in experimental physics, Marco has led multiple projects and contributed to groundbreaking studies on optical spectroscopy and molecular interactions.
